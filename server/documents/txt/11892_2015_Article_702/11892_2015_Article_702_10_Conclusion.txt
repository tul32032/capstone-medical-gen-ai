Multidisciplinary Care and Partnership with Patient Organizations

Wolfram syndrome affects different organs and systems in the body. Thus, multidisciplinary care by physicians and healthcare professionals from a range of disciplines is required. Based on our experience, a strong patient-doctor partnership can facilitate the communication between physicians in different specialties.
