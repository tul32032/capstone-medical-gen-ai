We confirm that post-pituitary surgery copeptin measurement is useful to exclude DI early post-operatively and propose it as a potential tool to identify patients suitable for early discharge. It will be important to conduct future work on earlier sampling times, with real-time assay, and combine these results with other clinical and biochemical parameters to provide a robust dataset at 24 h

post-pituitary surgery enabling selection of patients for safe early discharge.
