Table 3 outlines the copeptin results for the DI versus non-DI group at each time point. As expected, pre-operative copeptin levels did not differ significantly between the two groups. A difference is evident at day 1 post-operatively, with copeptin levels being lower in the DI group, and this is also seen at day 8 and 6 weeks post-operatively. There is no significant difference seen at day 2, largely due to the presence of an outlier result. The copeptin results are displayed graphically in Fig. 1. We observed that, at day 1, a copeptin level of less than 3.4 pmol/l is 91.0% sensitive for detecting DI but only 55.2% specific.
